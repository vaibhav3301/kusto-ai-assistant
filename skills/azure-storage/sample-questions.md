# Azure Storage - Sample Questions

Questions you can ask when the Azure Storage skill is loaded.

---

## Capacity & Usage

> "What are the top 10 storage accounts by capacity?"

> "Show me the total storage capacity trend over the last month"

> "What does the blob age distribution look like?"

> "How many storage accounts have more than 1 TB of data?"

---

## Transactions & Performance

> "Show daily transaction counts for the past 2 weeks"

> "Which accounts have the highest transaction volumes?"

> "What's the hourly transaction pattern - are there peak hours?"

---

## Security

> "Are there any anonymous access requests in the last 24 hours?"

> "Show me authentication failures by account"

> "Which operations are most commonly accessed anonymously?"

---

## Critical Customers

> "Which subscriptions are flagged as critical customers?"

> "Show me the critical customer list with their priority levels"

> "Are any critical customers experiencing issues?"
