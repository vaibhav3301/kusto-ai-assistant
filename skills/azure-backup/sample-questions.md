# Azure Backup - Sample Questions

Here are questions you can ask the Kusto AI Assistant when the Azure Backup
skill is loaded. Just type these (or similar) in Copilot Chat.

---

## Customer & Usage Questions

> "How many unique subscriptions are using ADLS backup in the last 7 days?"

> "Show me the top 20 backup customers by job count this week"

> "Which Azure regions have the most backup customers?"

> "What types of workloads are being backed up? Show me a breakdown."

> "How many vaults are actively running backups?"

---

## Health & Reliability Questions

> "What's the overall backup success rate this week?"

> "Show me the daily backup success rate trend for the last 30 days"

> "Which vaults have the highest failure rates?"

> "Are there any subscriptions with more than 50% failure rate?"

> "Show me backup failures in UAE North region today"

---

## Trend & Analysis Questions

> "How has the number of backup customers grown over the last 4 weeks?"

> "Compare backup job counts across all 3 clusters (prod1, weu, wus)"

> "Show me ADLS vs Blob backup job volumes side by side"

> "What day of the week has the most backup activity?"

---

## Investigation Questions

> "Find the subscription with vault name 'bkv-stg-css-01' and show all their jobs"

> "Show me all failed ADLS backup jobs in the last 24 hours with error details"

> "Which subscriptions started using ADLS backup for the first time this week?"

> "Is there a specific region where failures are increasing?"
